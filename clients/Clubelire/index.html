Attuned to Luxury

We are India’s first Private Air Travel Club offering Private Jet Flights to various destinations in India. Our members are Frequent Travelers (Business and Leisure).

Experience our Hassle Free flying experience that saves valuable time and provides you a chance to meet like-minded successful people; helping you to network in the skies. To begin with we have started flying to and from Mumbai, Delhi, Pune, Surat and Ahmedabad, with additional destinations such as Indore, Bhopal, Gwalior, Bengaluru and Nagpur to follow. Club Elire intends to help the frequent traveler reduce the time and pain of Travel.

Club Elire is an exclusive community of successful professionals who can travel together, and become close business associates in due course of time. A meeting can be commenced at take-off and a deal struck before landing.
